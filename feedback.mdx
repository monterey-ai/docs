### Provide Feedback

Your insights are crucial to us, and we've made it easy for you to share your thoughts:

**Direct Feedback**: Notice the "Feedback" button on the bottom right corner of  Monterey? Click on it, type in your comments, and hit submit. It's quick and efficient!

**Slack Community**: Not only can you engage with fellow users in our Slack community, but our entire team is there too. This means you can directly converse with us, ensuring your feedback reaches the right ears and fosters genuine conversations.
