### Overview

Send feedback directly into Asana as tasks so your team can track, triage, and take action—all without copy-pasting between tools.

### Set up

Go to the integration settings page and select the Asana integration. Follow the guidelines to connect your Asana account. Only admins of your workspace can set up Asana integration.
