### Set up

Use Zapier to automatically forward emails to Reforge Insight Analytics in order to establish a transparent pipeline for the team. For many businesses, emails are the primary means of receiving feedback and addressing customer inquiries. 

Follow the steps in the Loom video to set up the ingestion pipeline: https://www.loom.com/share/4e03d851f0cd4f8798ad5ed3be789238
