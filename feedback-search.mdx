### Filter, Search & Display**

- **Filtering**: Streamline your view by applying filters such as creation time, category, priority, and more. You can even get granular with metadata filters like URL or username.
- **Search**: Not sure which filter to apply? Use the dedicated search bar at the top of the page, plugging in keywords to quickly find relevant feedback. (not online yet)
- **Display Preferences**: Personalize your view by adjusting the columns displayed. Choose from options like source, content, customer, category, projects, and created date.
