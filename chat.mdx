## Intro

Chat is a GPT-4 powered feedback analytics tool. You can ask the Monty AI chatbot any questions you have about the feedback data in your workspace. 
Monterey AI will find the most relevant feedback items to your question and send them along with your question to the GPT models, thus analyzing your feedback and generating a comprehensive answer to your question.
