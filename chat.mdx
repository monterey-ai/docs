## Intro

Chat is an GPT4 powered feedback analytics tool. You can ask the Monty AI chatbot any questions you have about the feedback data in your workspace. Monterey AI will find the most relevant feedback items to that question, then send the combo of that original question and those similar feedback items to GPT models. Thus analyze your feedback and generate a comprehensive answer to your question.
